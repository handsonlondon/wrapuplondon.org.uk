Wrap Up London is back

For the 7th year running, we are collecting your old, unwanted coats and giving them to charities that support the homeless, refugees, children living in poverty and people fleeing domestic violence (to name a few).

Help us keep vulnerable people warm this winter by donating a coat this November!

Host a collection

Whether it’s at your school, place of work, place of worship, favourite club, local store, or your neighbourhood, why not host your own collection?

How to host a collection:

1. Assemble your team – ask your friends, family and colleagues to get involved and spread the word.

2. Collect the coats – we take all kinds! We launch on the 13 November 2017, but feel free to choose your own start date.

3. Send the coats – either in-person or via post:

Chiswick Safestore
22 Sutton Lane North,
Great West Road,
Chiswick,
W4 4LD

Most importantly, don't forget to have fun! Use the #WrapUpLondon hashtag on social media and celebrate your own #HandsOnHeroes!

All coats must be donated by 24 November 2017.

Donate Money

It costs as little as £3 to collect and distribute a warm coat. Your morning latte could help keep someone warm all winter.

Text HLDN14 followed by your donation amount £# (e.g HLDN14 £5) to 70070.
