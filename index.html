Wrap Up London 2020

Wrap Up London week 2020 will run from Monday 9th - Wednesday 11th November.

In this difficult and challenging year, it’s even more important to us to support the most vulnerable people in the capital through Wrap Up London this winter. We’re working hard on exactly what we need to do to make sure that everything about the campaign is completely Covid-19 safe.

Keep watching this space (and our Twitter and Instagram pages) where we will confirm as soon as we can all the information you need to donate your coat. Our collection points and sorting sessions will look very different this year, and will of course be in line with any government recommendations.

If you’re interested in volunteering or even running your own local Wrap Up, please get in touch!
