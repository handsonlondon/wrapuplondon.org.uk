The Wrap Up Family

From our first limited London coat collection in 2011, Hands On London has inspired other organisations to grow our Wrap Up Family.

In 2019 we welcome back to the campaign: Wrap Up Central Manchester, Wrap Up Birmingham and Wrap Up Glasgow in partnership with Human Appeal; Wrap Up Newport, in association with Help the Homeless Newport and Pride in PIL, and Wrap Up Lancashire with the Rotary Club. This year we're delighted to welcome the following organisations and coat collections to the Wrap Up Family:

  • Wrap Up Bath, run in association with the University of Bath Student volunteering Society and The Genesis Trust
  • Wrap Up Brighton & Hove, run in partnership with the Rotary Club and AMEX
  • Wrap Up Cardiff, run in association with Prisoners’ Education Trust
  • Wrap Up campaigns in Cumbria, Essex and Greater Manchester, run in partnership with the Rotary Club
  • Wrap Up Leicester, run in partnership with Human Appeal.
  • Wrap Up Wellingborough, run in association with Kuehne+Nagel and Daylight Centre Fellowship
  • Wrap Up Uxbridge, run in association with Love Uxbridge and Trinity Homeless Projects
  • Let's keep growing and widening the support of the Wrap Up Family! If you’re inspired to start your own Wrap Up campaign, get in touch!
